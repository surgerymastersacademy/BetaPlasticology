🌟 Overview

Welcome to Plasticology Ultimate Edition, a comprehensive, all-in-one web application designed specifically for plastic surgery residents, fellows, and specialists. Our platform integrates high-yield content, advanced testing tools, and performance analytics to streamline your learning process and ensure you are exam-ready.

Our motto is "Together We Can," and we believe that by providing the right tools, we can help you achieve your professional goals.

✨ Core Features

Plasticology is packed with features tailored to the needs of modern surgical education.

🧠 MCQ Bank (بنك الأسئلة)

A massive, continuously updated question bank designed to test your knowledge across the entire spectrum of plastic surgery.

  • Exam Simulation Mode: Experience a real exam environment with a fixed number of questions and a global timer. No hints, no immediate feedback—just you and the test.
  • Custom Mock Exams: Create personalized quizzes based on the number of questions, time per question, specific chapters, or even sources.
  • Topic Search: Instantly generate a quiz on any topic you want to focus on, such as "Flaps," "Burns," or "Cleft Lip."
  • Practice Modes: Practice your mistakes or revisit bookmarked questions.

👨‍⚕️ OSCE Bank (بنك الحالات الإكلينيكية)

Prepare for your clinical exams with our interactive OSCE module, featuring real-world scenarios, images, and follow-up questions in both MCQ and essay formats.

📺 Lectures

Access a structured library of video lectures covering the most important topics in plastic surgery, organized by chapter for easy navigation.

📚 Learning Mode (وضع المذاكرة)

A stress-free way to study. Browse questions with the correct answers and rationales immediately visible, allowing you to learn and revise without exam pressure.

📂 Resource Library

A curated collection of essential books, landmark papers, and important resources, all accessible with a single click to supplement your learning.

📈 Performance Tracking & Analytics

Your personal dashboard to monitor your educational journey with a detailed activity log, comprehensive statistics, and a competitive leaderboard.

🛠️ Other Smart Features

  • Note-Taking: Add personal notes to any lecture or quiz question and review them later from a central "My Notes" section.
  • Plasticology Radio: Listen to educational content while you work or commute, directly within the app.
  • Announcements: Stay updated with the latest additions, features, and important news.
  • Cross-Platform: Fully functional on both desktop and mobile browsers.

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Login: Access the application and log in. You can also try a free sample test.
  2. Explore the Main Menu: The main menu is your central hub to navigate to all features.
  3. Start Learning: Jump into any mode that suits your study style for the day.
  4. Track Your Progress: Visit the Activity Log to see your performance and identify areas for improvement.

💡 For an Optimal Experience

  • For focused study sessions like the Exam Simulator or OSCEs, using a desktop or laptop computer is recommended.
  • Use the "Bookmark" feature during quizzes to mark questions you want to review thoroughly later.
  • Regularly use the "Practice Mistakes" mode—it's one of the most effective ways to turn weaknesses into strengths.
